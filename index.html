Biography

Gary Clark, Jr. (born February 15, 1984) is an American guitarist and actor based in Austin, Texas. Clark shared the stage with various legends of rock and roll, and has stated that he is "influenced by blues, jazz, soul country, as well as hip hop". Clark's musical trademarks are his distorted guitar sound and smooth vocal style.
